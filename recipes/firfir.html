FIRFIR

A Fine Enjera FIRFIR

Enjera firfir is one of the casual dishes of Ethiopia. It is easily prepared and widely consumed in almost all parts of Ethiopia.

Firfir is prepared from Injera and casually used as a common breakfast dish or a brunch/snack. As it also is fairly easier to prepare, it is preferred by many people.

INGREDIENTS

  • Enjera
  • Edible oil
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

STEPS

  1. Chop two pieces of Onion
  2. cook the chopped onion with a spoon of edible oil.
  3. chop two pieces of a regular sized tomato and cook it with the Onion.
  4. add two spoon chilli powder
  5. Once cooked well, add water and wait for it to boil.
  6. Once the water boils, add some salt and butter and take it off from the stove.
  7. then chop an Injera of the desired amount and mix it with the boiled sauce water. Then it is ready to be served.
