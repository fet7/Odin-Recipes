SHIRO

A Fine Habeshan SHIRO

Kitfo is a traditional lovely dish of Ethiopian people. It is a widely known dish all over the country. It is a casual daily dish for most Ethiopians.

Shiro is made of primarily roasted yellow peas powder. It looks some kind of porridge though not as thick. Shiro is commonly consumed with Enjera, another Ethiopian local flat bread, which looks like a spongy fermented pancake.

INGREDIENTS

  • Onion
  • Edible oil
  • Tomato
  • Chilli Powder
  • yellow peas powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

STEPS

  • Once boiled,add some yellow peas powder until the mix becomes thicker like a soup.
  • add some salt and finally add a half spoon butter. Then let it boil well and it is ready.
