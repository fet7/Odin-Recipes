KITFO

A Fine Habeshan KITFO

Kitfo is a traditional lovely dish of Ethiopian people. It originates from Guraghe people but is popular in cities and towns across the country. It is also considered a traditional dish Among people of Wolayta, Silte, Gamo and other neighboring communities.

KItfo is made of raw beef. The dish is famously enjoyed on holidays and celebrations. It is not common to prepare it casually even if there is no cultural boundary that prohibits as such. Generally, it is considered a special dish for special occasions.

INGREDIENTS

  • Raw Beef
  • Butter
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Cardamon Powder

STEPS

  1. grind down a 1/4kg of raw pure red beef
  2. Add a tea cup of molten Habesha butter. Add the butter until the beef is well soaked by the butter.
  3. Add two spoon chilli powder and mix it well.
  4. add a teaspoon of salt and a teaspoon of black cardamon powder and mix it well.
